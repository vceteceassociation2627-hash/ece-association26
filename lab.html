Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering

LaboratoriesWhere Circuits Meet Curiosity

Seven dedicated labs equip every ECE student with hands-on experience across communication systems, VLSI, embedded design and beyond — backed by DRDO, AICTE and DST funded infrastructure.

Infrastructure

Our Laboratories

RF & Comms

Communication Systems Lab

5G Simulation • SDR Technology • Wireless Innovation Zone

The Communication Systems Laboratory provides students with practical exposure to modern communication technologies, including analog and digital transmission techniques. The lab is equipped with advanced tools that enable experimentation with modulation, multiplexing, and signal propagation. Students gain hands-on experience in analyzing real-time signals and understanding wireless communication concepts.

  • Signal Generators
  • Spectrum Analyzers
  • Antenna Trainer Kits
DSP

Digital Signal Processing Lab

MATLAB & Python • Real-time Signal Processing • Audio Analysis Setup

The Digital Signal Processing (DSP) Laboratory focuses on the analysis and processing of signals using modern computational tools. Students work with platforms like MATLAB and Python to design filters, perform spectral analysis, and process real-time audio and image signals. The lab provides a strong foundation in understanding signal behavior and system performance.

  • TMS320 DSP Kits
  • MATLAB Workstations
  • CRO & Function Generators
VLSI

VLSI Design Lab

MATLAB & Python • Real-time Signal Processing • Audio Analysis Setup

The VLSI Design Laboratory offers an advanced environment for learning integrated circuit design and semiconductor technologies. Students are trained in using industry-standard Electronic Design Automation (EDA) tools for circuit simulation, synthesis, and layout design. The lab emphasizes practical implementation through FPGA prototyping and chip-level design projects.

  • Xilinx FPGA Boards
  • Cadence / Modelsim
  • High-end Workstations
Embedded Core

Microprocessor & Microcontroller Lab

Embedded Systems • ARM Kits • Robotics Integration

The Microprocessor and Microcontroller Laboratory provides hands-on experience with programming and interfacing of microprocessors and microcontrollers. Students work with kits such as 8085/8086 and modern controllers like ARM and PIC to develop embedded applications. The lab focuses on real-time problem-solving, hardware interfacing, and system design.

  • 8085 / 8086 Kits
  • ARM & PIC Trainers
  • Interfacing Modules
Devices & Circuits

Electronic Devices & Circuits Lab

Smart Workbenches • Circuit Simulation • Practical Innovation Space

The Electronic Devices and Circuits Laboratory introduces students to the fundamentals of electronic components and circuit behavior. Through practical experiments, students study the characteristics of diodes, transistors, and amplifiers. The lab emphasizes hands-on learning using modern workbenches and simulation tools.

  • Regulated Power Supplies
  • Breadboard Stations
  • Component Kits
Analog / Digital

Analog & Digital Electronics Lab

Logic Design • PCB Prototyping • Mixed Signal Testing

The Analog and Digital Electronics Laboratory focuses on the design and analysis of both analog and digital circuits. Students perform experiments on logic gates, combinational and sequential circuits, and analog systems. The lab is equipped with trainer kits and testing instruments that enable practical learning.

  • Logic Gate Trainers
  • ADC / DAC Kits
  • Digital IC Trainers
IoT & Embedded

IoT & Embedded Systems Lab

IoT Devices • Cloud Integration • Smart Automation Projects

The IoT and Embedded Systems Laboratory is a modern innovation space where students design and develop smart devices using microcontrollers, sensors, and cloud platforms. The lab focuses on real-time data acquisition, automation, and connectivity.

  • Raspberry Pi & Arduino
  • Sensor Node Kits
  • Wi-Fi / IoT Modules
