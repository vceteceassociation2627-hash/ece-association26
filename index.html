Leadership

Chairman's Message

Every young Indian of this great country should dream big. It is not enough if they only dream — they should work hard to make it a reality. At Velammal College of Engineering and Technology, Madurai, we provide the necessary platform for aspiring youth of this region to become enterprising engineers, so they can deliver the right engineering solutions to propel our nation forward.

We sincerely believe in imparting quality engineering education laced with deep social values, ensuring every graduate becomes not only a competent engineer but also a responsible citizen. It is time we moved from "standardized testing" to "creative teaming." Life offers no warranties or guarantees — only possibilities and opportunities. We want our budding engineers to remember this and make the best use of them. Please join us in this exhilarating journey.

Shri M.V. Muthuramalingam, Chairman

Principal's Message

"Branding is the art of becoming knowable, likable and trustable," as John Jantsch puts it — and VCET is a known, liked and trusted technical institution in Madurai. I'm proud to be part of an institution that keeps me creative, analytical, and innovative.

Department

HOD's Message

A hearty welcome to the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at Velammal College of Engineering and Technology, Madurai. Since 2007, the department has been a front-runner in imparting quality technical education. A rich technical ambience, enthusiastic faculty, state-of-the-art laboratories, and strong management support have helped students perform with distinction.

The department is also making strides in Research and Development, having procured funded projects from DRDO, AICTE, and DST worth ₹258.716 lakhs in just three years. Our students have been placed in top MNCs including TCS, CTS, Wipro, Accenture, Aricent, HP, UST Global, Zoho, and Athena Healthcare, supported by a vibrant worldwide alumni network.

Foundations

Vision, Mission & Outcomes

Vision

To emerge and sustain as a center of excellence for technical and managerial education upholding social values.

Mission

  1. Impart comprehensive, innovative and value-based education.
  2. Expose students to technical, managerial and soft-skill resources with emphasis on research and professionalism.
  3. Inculcate the need for a disciplined, happy, and peaceful life.

PEOs & PSOs

PEO I: Graduates will have a high level of technical competency to identify problems and produce innovative solutions for industrial needs.

PEO II: Graduates will adapt to changes in roles and responsibilities through professional development and lifelong learning.

PEO III: Graduates will ethically apply their computing knowledge considering societal, economic and environmental factors.

PSO I: Apply mathematics, science and engineering knowledge to solve complex IT problems, designing and developing solutions using modern tools.

PSO II: Apply technology, ethics, management principles and soft skills to carry out societal IT projects as a team member and leader.
